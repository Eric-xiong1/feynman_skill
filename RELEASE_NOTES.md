# Release Notes

## v0.1.0

Feynman Skill v0.1.0 turns the original Feynman Translator method into a clean, standalone agent skill.

Highlights:

- Paste confusing academic or technical text and get a direct Chinese explanation.
- Default output includes plain-language explanation, life analogy and jargon translation.
- Switch audience or style through natural language instead of menus.
- Ask for knowledge trees, deeper follow-up questions or self-test feedback when needed.

Recommended launch positioning:

```text
一个中文优先的费曼解释 skill：把论文、教材和技术黑话翻译成人话。
```
